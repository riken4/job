About Miteri Mart

"Bringing quality, connection, and community to your doorstep."

Our Story

Born out of a simple idea—to create a local marketplace that feels like a friendly neighbor—Miteri Mart is more than just a store. The word **"Miteri"** comes from Nepali, meaning **"friendship"**, and that's the spirit we infuse into everything we do. We started small, with a handful of dedicated local artisans and farmers, and have grown into a trusted online destination for curated goods that are both high-quality and ethically sourced.

Our Commitment

We believe that shopping should be a joyful and meaningful experience. That's why we meticulously select every item in our catalog, focusing on products that tell a story. From handcrafted accessories to fresh, organic produce, each item at Miteri Mart represents our commitment to excellence and our support for small-scale creators. We're dedicated to building a community where customers and creators connect, fostering a cycle of trust and mutual respect.

Our Values

Our core values guide every decision we make:

Quality

We never compromise on quality. Every product is carefully vetted to ensure it meets our high standards.

Community

We prioritize supporting local businesses and artisans, helping their passion projects thrive.

Trust

We operate with transparency and honesty, building a relationship of trust with our customers.

Sustainability

We are committed to offering products that are environmentally friendly and promote a sustainable lifestyle.

Thank you for being a part of the Miteri Mart family. Your support helps us continue our mission of building a better, more connected marketplace for everyone.
